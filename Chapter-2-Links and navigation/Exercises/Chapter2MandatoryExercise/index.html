Many Missions

From simple missions to the advanced, Kerbal Space Program offers a variety of missions to try. These missions are especially important in different game modes, which will be introduced later.

Build Your Own Spacecraft

Kerbal Space Program features a wide range of parts. Depending on the DLCs you own, you can access parts that resemble real-life spacecraft. These parts allow you to build spacecraft tailored to specific missions.

Explore Different Worlds

Just like our solar system, Kerbal Space Program features multiple worlds to explore. While they resemble real-life planets, they have unique names and characteristics.

Expand Your Reach

As you explore more worlds, you can expand your reach by utilizing their resources. This allows for more capable spacecraft and enhances your gameplay experience.

Other Modes of Play

Kerbal Space Program offers various game modes that determine how you play. For example, in one mode, you start with basic parts and use science to unlock advanced parts while managing funds to finance your spacecraft.
