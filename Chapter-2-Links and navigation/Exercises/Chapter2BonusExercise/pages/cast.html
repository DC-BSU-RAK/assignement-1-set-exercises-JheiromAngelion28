Cast

Kerbal Space Program features a variety of characters, including the Kerbals, who are the main focus of the game. While there is no traditional cast, the game's development team at Squad played a crucial role in bringing the Kerbals to life. One of the most popular characters are Jebediah, Bill, And Valentina. They are the ones that appear first when you fly your rockets
