Plot

Kerbal Space Program is a space flight simulation game where you take control of a space program for an alien race known as the Kerbals. Your goal is to build spacecraft, explore the universe, and complete missions while managing resources and overcoming challenges. If you want to keep advancing, there are other Mods that can be used to increase the dificulty of the game.
