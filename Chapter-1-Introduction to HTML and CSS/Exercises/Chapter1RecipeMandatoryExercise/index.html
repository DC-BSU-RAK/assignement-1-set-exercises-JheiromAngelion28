How to make a Beef Wellington

My favorite recipe page

Beef Wellington is a dish commonly served in high end restaurants. It is a complex dish that is master by many british chefs making itvery known in the cooking world. It demands a lot of preparation and technique to get it right, especially when this dish has a beef inside a puff pastry which will contain the meat, making the margin for error very small. We will require certain ingredients that must be used in order to complete the dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of Tenderloin Beef.
  • Salt and Black Paper.
  • 2 table spoons of Virgin olive oil.
  • 2 Table spoons of mustard.
  • 1 Pound of Mushrooms.
  • 4 slices of ham.
  • 4 thin slices of Puff Pastry.
  • 2 egg yolks that are beaten and mixed.
  • Flaky salt to sprinkle.

The Cooking method

  1. Preheat theoven to 400 degrees F as you assemble the whellington.
  2. The beef must be seared for about 2 to 3 minutes on each side.
  3. Spread Mustard on the beef.
  4. Prepare and chop mushrooms, cook them t relears moisture for 10/15 minutes.
  5. Wrap and place the mushrooms paste into the beef and ham.
  6. Wrap the beef with puff pastry.
  7. Do an egg wash using the beten eggs.
  8. Bake on the ovem at 400F for 25/35 minutes
  9. Serve the dish
